Sheep-o-tastic Server Wiki

Hello everyone! Welcome to the Sheep-o-tastic Wiki. Sheep-o-tastic is a Casual Survival Minecraft server with lots to do and a friendly community. As such, most people would need some form of guidance for all the new plugins and systems (some of them custom to our server) on our server. We have loads of content that keep the gameplay fresh and unlike any other server. You can join our server at Sheepotastic.vsmc.co .

Plugins and Mechanics

Survival Plugins

These are plugins that deepen the survival experience of our server. Most have to be turned on and are purely optional, so you can have as hard, or as easy, of an experience as you want (whenever you want). (Thirst, Diseases, SurvivalMechanics, etc.)

Artisan Plugins

Use these plugins to bring out your inner artisan, Creating things for the sake of creation and beauty. Make Art that you can sell and frame, or brew up a cold drink to share with friends are your own pub. Decorate your home with unique furniture. (Artmap, Brewery, and DiceFurniture)

Server Systems

Races

Choose from 5 different races to start off on the server, each one having 3 passive skills (1 of which you can use with crouching), 2 negative passive skills, and 2 skills they can use at will!

Races
Classes

This system will be announced at a later date, it will work similarly to races but with less passives and more skills with levels.

Difficulty Settings

Our server contains a warp where you can specifically set if certain plugins affect you, for if you're wanting a bit of an extra challenge. This can include Diseases, Thirst, Keepinventory, and more!
