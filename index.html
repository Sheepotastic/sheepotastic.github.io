    OUR PLUGINS

    Slimefun

    Slimefun adds loads of mod-like content within vanilla minecraft. Scriptable robots, Backpacks, Ore processing, enhanced furnaces, talismans that give buffs, and many more! Adds potentially dozens of hours of content and fun to the server!

    Let out your Artistic Side!

    With plugins like Artmap, Brewery, and DiceFurniture, you can let out your artisan side and make paintings you can frame in your home or sell to others. With brewery, you can make up some alcohol (Or loads of other custom drinks) and drink with friends! (Blurred vision and slurred speech included!) Or Decorate your home with loads of custom (and functional) furniture!

    Optional Difficulty settings!

    Yes, you read that correctly. By default, things are chill and keepinventory is on. However, if you want to, you can make things more difficult by turning on Thirst, Diseases, turn off keepinventory, and even remove /back on death for yourself, for those that one a more challenging survival experience! (Only yourself, of course)

    Classes and Races

    At the start of your adventure, you can pick a Race and Class, both of which giving you special skills and passive perks. Races even give an additional function to when you crouch for even more variety!

    Literally too much to list!

    We have so many plugins that add more content and things to do that I would pretty much fill thise page listing the content like this. We also have loads of custom, this server only, content such as custom scripts, brewery drinks, custom classes/races, Custom recipes, Throwable weapons, and even more!
