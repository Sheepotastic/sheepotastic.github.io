    Craftbook

    Craftbook is a large plugin that can be seen as changing 2 things: 1. Adding various mechanics that can be used by the player in order to avoid using redstone (automatic creation of elevators, doors, gates, etc.) 2. Adding various smaller mechanics to gameplay. We're gonna clarify how to use these mechanics first, then the smaller stuff after.

    Mechanic Name Description Usage Limitations
    Bridge The player can create toggleable bridges that can be removed by right clicking a sign or by powering the sign with redstone. Typing "[Bridge]" on the 2nd line of a sign and doing so again on the opposite side of the bridge. Restricted to: Cobblestone, Wood, Glass, Wood Slabs, or Stone slabs.
    Door The player can create a toggleable door that, when right clicking or powering the sign with redstone, removes/places the blocks in the doorway Typing "[Door Up]" on the 2nd line of a sign, on the bottom middle section of the door and "[Door Down]" (2nd line) on a sign on the upper middle section. Restricted to: Cobblestone, Glass, Wood, Wood slabs, and Stone slabs.
    Elevator The player can create a right click-able sign that can teleport them up or down to different sections of their home. Typing "[Elevator Up]" (2nd line) on a sign, on the lower floor and "[Elevator Down]" (2nd line) on a sign on the upper floor. They must be at the exact same coordinate except their height. -
    Gate The player can create a toggleable sign that can close and open a fenced off area located near the sign. Can also be toggled with redstone powering the sign. Typing "[Gate]" (2nd line) on a sign, near a completely filled in door-way. Restricted to: Fence, Glass Pane, and Iron Bars.
    Hidden Switch The player can create a block that can be right clicked to toggle a button or lever on the opposite side. Typing "[x]" (2nd line) on a sign that is next to a button or lever. When the block with the sign is right-clicked, it will activate the object. -
    Mechanic Name Description Usage
    AI Mechanics This allows zombies to be sneaked passed in low light, Makes Skeletons occassionally crit (catches the player on fire), and Zombies attack passive mobs. -
    Better Physics This causes ladders to fall when placed rather than stick to the wall.
    Bookcase When bookcases are right-clicked, they give a random blurb of text that can be interesting to read.
    Jukebox/Jackolantern/Netherrack When carved pumpkins are powered by redstone, they become jackolanterns. Jukeboxes will replay the song in them when repowered. Netherrack will become lit when powered by redstone.
    Painting Switcher When a painting is placed, the player can right click the painting and scroll or use the hotbar to switch the painting to another.
    XP Storer When an Emerald block is shift+Right-clicked, it will store the user's current exp into bottles at a minor loss.
