Survival Mechanics

This plugin allows you to have better control over your player's movements. It allows things such as crawling, wall climbing, wall kicks, sliding, and more. It takes a while to get used to, but once you're used to it, it improves exploration and general movement greatly.

Movement type Controls Description
Crawl Double tap sneak while on the ground This allows you to sneak along the ground, even entering 1 block spaces.
Slide Double tap sneak while running You quickly slide across the ground, this is able to be used in 1 block spaces as well.
Wallkick Press sneak while looking at a wall and jumping Kick off the wall facing you, sending you upward and backwards.
Roof Hang Right click the block above you while jumping (Empty handed) Use this to prevent yourself from dropping into a potentially dangerous situation
Climb Right click the wall infront of you while jumping Allows you to climb as if there were vines there, against any flat wall.
Mobkick Jump and sneak while looking at a mob in close range Does minimal damage but knocks back nearby mobs, allowing for more spacing.
