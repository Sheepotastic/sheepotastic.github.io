    Survival Plugins

    These are plugins that deepen the survival experience of our server. Most have to be turned on and are purely optional, so you can have as hard, or as easy, of an experience as you want (whenever you want). (Thirst, Diseases, SurvivalMechanics, etc.)

    Artisan Plugins

    Use these plugins to bring out your inner artisan, Creating things for the sake of creation and beauty. Make Art that you can sell and frame, or brew up a cold drink to share with friends are your own pub. Decorate your home with unique furniture. (Artmap, Brewery, and DiceFurniture)
