    Thirst

    This plugin must be enabled in order to work, you can access it at /warp difficulty. Thirst is, well, a thirst system. It adds an additional difficulty to surviving in the minecraft world. It adds a lot of realism to the server as well as difficulty. Thirst can be satiated by dipping in water, eating foods with high water quantity (watermelon), or drinking water bottles. It works well with another one of our difficulty options: Diseases. The thirst rate rises when in hotter biomes (desert, nether, etc.). Water bottles stack for additional storage, and the plugin will ignore AFK players.

    If you have both Thirst and Diseases enabled, You must cook water bottles before drinking or you risk contracting cholera. If just thirst is enabled, you can drink from any water source freely.
    Recipe Description
    Surrounding a waterbottle with snowballs will produce a drinkable water bottle.
    Ensure you put any dirty water into a furnace to clean the water before drinking if also using Diseases.
    Egg Potion Putting an egg above an empty bottle will produce an egg potion which can reduce thirst.
    Edible sources Eating: Mushroom soup, Rabbit Stew, Melons, or Apples and drinking potions or milk buckets will reduce thirst as well.
    Commands Usage/Details
    /thirst Using this will display your current thirst level (0-100, you start taking damage at 0 and experience negative effects around 20). Chat will warn you of low thirst starting at 20% or lower.
