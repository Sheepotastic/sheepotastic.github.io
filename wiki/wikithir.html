Thirst

This plugin allows players to experience a more realistic and challenging survival experience. You'll constantly have a thirst bar and it will require you to drink (preferably untainted) water.
You're able to check your thirst at any time via /thirst. In addition, the plugin will warn you in chat when you're beginning to get dangerously low on thirst. If you run out of thirst, you'll begin to take damage.
This plugin must be enabled in order to use, at /warp difficulty. Otherwise it doesn't apply to players. It can be disabled at any time.

If you have both Thirst and Diseases enabled, You must cook water bottles before drinking or you risk contracting cholera. If just thirst is enabled, you can drink from any water source freely.

Things to keep in mind:

  • Thirst reduces gradually over time. When in certain biomes, it will decrease more (Such as the nether, savannahs, and deserts).
  • Thirst will ignore players who are AFK or haven't enabled the plugin for themselves.
  • Player starts to gain negative effects as the thirst level gets really low, such as weakness, fatigue, or nausea.
  • Water bottles now stack, giving more inv space to store water.
