    Jukebox

    Jukebox is an in-game music player. Simply use /music and it will bring up a list and options for the music we have. All music is played via noteblocks. If you wish, you can also suggest new music for the jukebox in our discord server so long as you have a .midi or .nbs file of the song you want added. Warning: Sometimes the music can lag a bit, especially depending on the location of the player.

    Commands Usage/Details
    /music Brings up all options for the music, including a music list, volume settings, and auto-play settings.
