    Diseases

    This plugin must be enabled in order to work, you can access it at /warp difficulty. This plugin introduces an array of diseases the player can recieve due to being in certain environments or being near other sick players. Theses diseases can all be cured by crafting a particular potion. Immunity can be built up to a particular disease by curing it or taking a vaccine against it. The diseases all cause varying negative effects and the effects worsen as the condition does. Being in hot areas can cause fatigue and confusion. Being in cold areas can cause pneumonia, slowness, or blindness. This plugin works well in tandom with Thirst and to create a more realistic and survival experience if the player so chooses.

    If you have both Thirst and Diseases enabled, You must cook water bottles before drinking or you risk contracting cholera. If just thirst is enabled, you can drink from any water source freely.
    Disease Symptoms Contraction
    Yellow Fever High Fever and sneezing. Contagious Being in Jungle Biomes.
    Whooping Cough Wheezing and Coughing. Being in Desert Biomes.
    Plague High Fever, Sneezing, Vomiting. Contagious Zombie Bite
    Cholera Vomiting Ingesting poluted water
    Broken Leg Slowness until mended Taking fall damage.
    Pneumonia Slowness, Reduced breath in water, Coughing. Extended stay in cold biomes.
    Rabies Fever, Vomiting, Confusion, speed, strength, heart failure. Bite from infected wolf
    The Pox Fever, Sneezing, vomiting, blindness, weakness. Contagious Being near too many animals.

    Fighting the Effects

    The harsher biomes and enviornments now mean that trips must be planned to some extent. The cold can be fought by making a campfire, being near lava, or simply carrying a heat source in hand (Torches, lava bucket, etc.). Warm milk can also help defeat the effects a bit by putting a milk bucket in the furnace, however, the milk will eventually cool down. The heat can be fought to a lesser extent, mainly by dipping into water or drinking cold water by putting snowballs with a bottle of water in a bench together. These will also warm up as time goes by so drink them quickly.

    Commands Usage/Details
    /disease check Check your current health status.
    /disease remedy list Show a list of crafting recipes to remedy any of the diseases.
    /temp This can be used to check the player's current condition.
