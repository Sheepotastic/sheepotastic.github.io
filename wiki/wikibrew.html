    Brewery

    Brewery is a plugin that allows the creation of various beverages. This plugin adds a distillation, Brewing, and aging method to create certain drinks. Alcholic drinks will slur speech, impede movement, and cause nausea and even vomiting in high amounts, however, they're loads of fun.
    When perfected, Drinks will give a colored name and have a positive prefix (Golden, pure, etc.). The name will vary with the quality of the drink, and so will the alcoholic content and negative/positive effects attached.

    Step 1 - Fermenting

    All drinks require fermenting. Place a cauldron over fire (or lava), fill it with water and place the ingredients inside. Wait the needed amount of time (you can check this by right clicking the cauldron with a clock). Right click it with empty bottles when its cooked long enough (3 bottles per batch).

    Step 2 - Distillation

    Not all drinks require this step. This is done by placing the drinks in a brewing stand with glowstone at the top box and fueled by blaze powder. The drinks will stop automatically when they've run through all of the distillation runs that they need to.

    Step 3 - Aging

    Not all drinks require this step. This can be made by placing the drinks into barrels. Barrels can be made by placing blocks in the style of either of the pictures shown below (the larger one stores more drinks) and typing "Barrel" onto the top lign of a sign. A message in chat will confirm that you've made the barrel. The type of wood used on the barrel can also effect the quality of the alcohol it outputs in some cases. Once every in-game day, the drinks will age by one year. You can check on them occassionally, and the colors will indicate when they're about to be ready. Some drinks may require just distilling, just aging, or both. Listed below are just a few of the many drinks you can create.

    Drink Name Ingredients Cook time Distill? Age? Effects
    Wheat-beer 3x Wheat 8 Min. - 2 Years, Birch Low Alcohol, No negative effects
    Beer 6x Wheat 8 Min. - 3 Years, Any wood Low Alcohol, No negative effects

    Vodka 10x Potatoes 15 Min. Yes - High Alcohol Content

    Coffee 12x Cocoa, 1x Milk Bucket 2 Min. - - Short Regen and Speed 1 for up to 140s.
    Watermelon Tequila 10x Watermelon Slice 15 Min. Yes - High Alcohol Content
    Confetti Wine 1x Cake 15 Min. Yes - High Alcohol
    Apple Cider 7x Apple, 2x Sugar 3 Min. Yes 7 Years, Birch Moderate Alcohol Content, No negative effects
    Commands Usage/Details
    /brew unlabel Strips off parts of the drink's lore that indicate how it was cooked. Drink keeps its name and effects.
    /brew info Shows how drunk the player is on a scale from 0-100 and what quality the alcohol was.
