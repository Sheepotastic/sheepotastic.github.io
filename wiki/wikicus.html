    Custom Recipes

    Our server has a large array of custom recipes which can make rarer or harder to find items easier to obtain (usually ones that maybe should be easier to obtain in the first place, like slimeballs). We don't want rdm or lucking across an untouched dungeon to be your only means of obtaining certain items like barding or saddles. Shapeless recipes
